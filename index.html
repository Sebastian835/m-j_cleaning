Our Story

M & J Cleaning Service LLC is a dedicated cleaning company owned and operated by Mireya Contreras. With more than three years of hands-on experience, we focus on delivering dependable and thorough cleaning services for offices, apartments, and commercial facilities.

We understand that cleanliness plays a key role in productivity, comfort, and first impressions. That is why our team is committed to maintaining high standards, using efficient cleaning methods, and adapting our services to the specific needs of each client.

Whether it is a first-time deep clean or ongoing janitorial service, we approach every job with professionalism, care, and consistency.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality cleaning services that create safe, clean, and comfortable environments for our clients while building long-term relationships based on trust and reliability.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a trusted cleaning partner for businesses and property managers by delivering consistent results, excellent service, and customized cleaning solutions.
